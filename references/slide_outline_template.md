# Prospect Audit & Strategy - Slide Deck Outline (SEO + GEO + AEO)

This document outlines the updated 16-20 slide structure for the client-facing presentation. The design must be premium, clean, modern, and aligned with TrafficRadius branding.

**CRITICAL:** Every slide with data MUST use a custom CSS diagram or visualization. **No bullet-point-only slides.**

---

### Slide 1: Title Slide

*   **Heading:** A Strategy for the New Age of Search
*   **Subheading:** An Integrated SEO, AEO & GEO Audit & Action Plan
*   **Client:** Prepared for [Prospect Name]
*   **Branding:** Prominent TrafficRadius logo.
*   **Footer:** © Copyright 2026 TrafficRadius, All rights reserved.

---

### Slide 2: The Search Landscape Has Changed

*   **Heading:** Beyond 10 Blue Links: The Three Layers of Modern Search
*   **Content:** A simple, educational overview of the new search ecosystem.
*   **Visualization:** A diagram with three concentric circles or columns:
    *   **Inner Circle/Column 1: SEO** (Classic Search Results)
    *   **Middle Circle/Column 2: AEO** (Answer Boxes, Featured Snippets)
    *   **Outer Circle/Column 3: GEO** (AI-Powered Answers - ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.)

---

### Slide 3: Your Digital Opportunity at a Glance

*   **Heading:** Your Current Multi-Layer Digital Snapshot
*   **Content:** Six large KPI metric cards summarizing the current state and opportunity.
*   **Visualization:** Six styled metric cards with icons:
    *   **Total Search Market (SEO):** Total monthly search volume.
    *   **Your Current Traffic (SEO):** Prospect's current organic traffic.
    *   **Answer Box Exposure (AEO):** % of top keywords where they appear in snippets/PAA.
    *   **AI Visibility Score (GEO):** A qualitative score (e.g., Low, Medium, High) based on entity strength and content structure.
    *   **Market Capture Rate (SEO):** Prospect's traffic as a % of total market.
    *   **Leader's Traffic (SEO):** Traffic of the #1 competitor.

---

### Slide 4: Competitive Landscape

*   **Heading:** How You Stack Up in the New Search Landscape
*   **Content:** A summary of the competitive analysis across SEO, AEO, and GEO.
*   **Visualization:** A multi-series horizontal bar chart comparing the prospect against 3-5 competitors on:
    *   Organic Traffic (SEO)
    *   Answer Engine Readiness Score (AEO/GEO)

---

### Slide 5: Integrated Audit Findings

*   **Heading:** Your Readiness for Modern Search
*   **Content:** A high-level summary of the most critical findings from the integrated audit.
*   **Visualization:** A dashboard-style visual with three sections (SEO, AEO, GEO), each with 2-3 key items and a Red/Amber/Green status.
    *   **SEO:** Page Speed, Indexability
    *   **AEO:** Schema Coverage, Content Structure
    *   **GEO:** AI Bot Accessibility, Entity Authority

---

### Slide 6: Uncovering Your Full Opportunity

*   **Heading:** Where Your Audience is Searching
*   **Content:** A breakdown of search demand by user intent.
*   **Visualization:** A donut or pie chart showing the split of keyword opportunities: **Commercial (SEO)**, **Question-Based (AEO)**, and **Informational/Conversational (GEO)**.

---

### Slide 7: Top Keyword Opportunities

*   **Content:** A table of the top 5-10 keyword opportunities.
*   **Visualization:** A styled table showing the keyword, search volume, and its primary layer (SEO, AEO, or GEO icon).
