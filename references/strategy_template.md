# Prospect Audit & Strategy Document Template (SEO + GEO + AEO)

This document outlines the updated structure for the integrated strategy document. The final output must be a comprehensive narrative that synthesizes all data into a compelling, three-layer (SEO, AEO, GEO) opportunity-focused story for the prospect.

---

## 1. Executive Summary

*   **Purpose:** A 1-page, high-level overview of the entire document.
*   **Content:**
    *   Start with a strong opening statement about the size of the *total* digital opportunity across classic search, answer engines, and AI-powered search.
    *   Summarize key findings from the integrated SEO, AEO, and GEO audit.
    *   Introduce the integrated three-pillar strategic approach.
    *   Conclude with a statement about the expected impact of becoming visible and authoritative across all three layers of search.

---

## 2. Understanding Modern Search: SEO, AEO & GEO

*   **Purpose:** To educate the prospect on the new search landscape and define the three layers of optimization.
*   **Content:**
    *   A simple, clear explanation of SEO, AEO, and GEO.
    *   A diagram illustrating how the three layers work together (traditional search, answer boxes, AI chat).

---

## 3. Your Current Digital Performance

*   **Purpose:** To establish a data-driven baseline of the prospect's current performance across all three layers.
*   **Content:**
    *   **SEO Performance:** Key SEMrush metrics (Domain Authority, Organic Traffic, Organic Keywords).
    *   **AEO Performance:** Current visibility in Featured Snippets and People Also Ask for top non-branded keywords.
    *   **GEO Performance:** An initial assessment of their brand's visibility and citation potential in AI search engines (based on entity authority and content structure).

---

## 4. Competitive Landscape

*   **Purpose:** To show the prospect where they stand against competitors across the modern search landscape.
*   **Content:**
    *   The main competitive comparison table, now including AEO/GEO indicators where possible.
    *   A visual chart comparing the prospect vs. competitors on metrics like Organic Traffic, Keywords, and an "Answer Engine Readiness" score.

---

## 5. Integrated Audit: SEO, AEO & GEO Opportunities

*   **Purpose:** To highlight the most critical issues and opportunities across all three layers.
*   **Content:**
    *   A summary of findings from `audit_findings.json`, grouped by layer:
        *   **SEO Findings:** Major indexability problems, slow page speed, on-page issues.
        *   **AEO Findings:** Lack of FAQ/HowTo schema, content not structured for direct answers.
        *   **GEO Findings:** AI bots blocked in `robots.txt`, weak entity signals, lack of `llms.txt`.

---

## 6. Holistic Opportunity Analysis

*   **Purpose:** To showcase the untapped search demand across different user intents.
*   **Content:**
    *   A chart showing the total addressable search demand, broken down by opportunity type: **Commercial (SEO)**, **Question-Based (AEO)**, and **Informational/Conversational (GEO)**.
    *   A table of the top 15-20 keyword opportunities, highlighting the type for each.

---

## 7. The Integrated SEO, AEO & GEO Strategy

*   **Purpose:** To outline the high-level, three-pillar strategic approach.
*   **Content:**
    *   Introduce the integrated three-pillar strategy:
        *   **Pillar 1: SEO Foundation & Authority:** Mastering traditional search.
        *   **Pillar 2: AEO & Answerability:** Becoming the definitive answer.
        *   **Pillar 3: GEO & AI Visibility:** Winning citations in generative AI.
    *   For each pillar, provide a short paragraph explaining the goal and key initiatives.

---

## 8. Pillar 1: SEO Foundation & Authority

*   **Purpose:** Detail the strategy for the SEO layer.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Technical SEO:** Key fixes for site health and crawlability.
    *   **On-Page SEO:** Optimizing existing pages for target commercial keywords.
    *   **Authority Building:** High-level plan for earning backlinks and mentions.

---

## 9. Pillar 2: AEO & Answerability

*   **Purpose:** Detail the strategy for the AEO layer.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Content for Answers:** Strategy for creating content that directly answers user questions (FAQs, guides).
    *   **Schema for Answers:** Plan for implementing `FAQPage`, `HowTo`, and other relevant schemas.
    *   **Voice Search Readiness:** Optimizing for conversational queries.

---

## 10. Pillar 3: GEO & AI Visibility

*   **Purpose:** Detail the strategy for the GEO layer.
*   **Content:**
    *   **AI Accessibility:** Ensuring all relevant AI crawlers can access the site.
    *   **Entity Optimization:** Strategy for strengthening brand and author entities.
    *   **Content for Citations:** Plan for creating citable, authoritative content (original research, data, expert insights).

---

## 11. Integrated 90-Day Action Plan

*   **Purpose:** To provide a clear, phased roadmap for the first three months.
*   **Content:**
    *   A table or visual timeline that breaks down the action plan into three 30-day phases, with integrated tasks for SEO, AEO, and GEO in each month.

---

## 12. Why TrafficRadius & Next Steps

*   **Purpose:** To reinforce the agency's value proposition and provide a clear call to action.
*   **Content:**
    *   Highlight the agency's expertise in the *full spectrum* of modern search.
    *   Clear "Next Steps" section.
